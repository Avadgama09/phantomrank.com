With 43% of B2B purchase research now beginning on AI platforms rather than traditional search engines, the rules of visibility have fundamentally changed. Agencies still selling keyword rankings are delivering reports their clients no longer care about. To prove real value, you must track the **AI visibility metrics** that actually dictate market share in 2026.

In this guide, you'll learn the 7 exact metrics your agency needs to track, how to calculate them, and why they are replacing traditional SEO KPIs.

## The Shift from Clicks to Citations

Search used to be about securing the number one spot and farming clicks. Today, AI engines like Perplexity, ChatGPT, and Gemini synthesize complete answers directly in the interface. For many queries, the click never happens. 

If your client isn't mentioned in the AI's synthesized response, they don't exist in the buyer's evaluation set. To track performance in a zero-click world, you need a new mathematical foundation.

## 1. AI Share of Voice (AI SOV)

Traditional Share of Voice tracked impression share on Google SERPs. **AI SOV** measures how often your client's brand is recommended within a specific topic area compared to their competitors.

*   **The Formula:** `(Your Brand Mentions + Citations) / (Total Brand Mentions + Citations for You + All Competitors) * 100`
*   **Why it matters:** Companies with strong AI SOV report 34% higher conversion rates and 28% shorter sales cycles. This directly replaces keyword impression share as your core competitive metric in client reporting.

## 2. Share of Synthesis (Primary Citation)

Getting cited is good; being the primary source of the AI's narrative is better. Share of Synthesis tracks the exact percentage of target buyer-intent prompts where your client successfully appears as the *first* or *primary* cited source.

*   **The Formula:** `(Prompts where your brand is primary source) / (Total target buyer-intent prompts tested) * 100`
*   **Why it matters:** Brands appearing as primary sources see 3.2x higher AI-referred conversion rates.

## 3. Citation Frequency & Coverage

Citation frequency tracks exactly how often your content is explicitly referenced and linked as a source of truth by an AI engine. 

*   **The Formula (Frequency):** `(Total times your domain is linked in AI answers) / (Total prompts tested) * 100`
*   **The Formula (Coverage):** `(Appearances with a clickable link) / (Total brand appearances) * 100`
*   **Why it matters:** 81% of AI-cited pages implement schema markup. Tracking this helps your agency prove the ROI of technical Answer Engine Optimization (AEO).

## 4. Brand Mention Rate (Inclusion Rate)

Unlike a direct citation, a brand mention occurs when an AI model names your client as a recommended solution in its conversational text without a clickable link.

*   **The Formula:** `(Prompts where brand is named or cited) / (Total prompts tested) * 100`
*   **Why it matters:** 65% of searches end without any click. Brand perception forms inside the AI answer itself. These unlinked mentions are your new top-of-funnel metric.

## 5. Narrative Accuracy & Sentiment

It doesn't matter if your client is mentioned if the AI gets their pricing wrong. Narrative Accuracy evaluates exactly how the AI characterizes the brand, the features it highlights, and whether it hallucinates incorrect information.

*   **What to track:** Sentiment Score (Positive/Neutral/Negative) and Hallucination Rate (%).
*   **Why it matters:** 23% of brand-related LLM queries contain factual inaccuracies. Agencies that monitor and correct these prevent massive reputational damage for their clients.

## 6. Branded Search Lift & Assisted Conversions

Because AI engines synthesize answers, users often don't click through immediately. Instead, they run a branded search on Google later.

*   **The Formula:** `(Branded keyword volume after AI visibility gain - Baseline branded volume) / (Baseline branded volume) * 100`
*   **Why it matters:** AI-referred traffic converts at 14.2% vs 2.8% for Google—a 5x premium. You must track these assisted conversions to prove true ROI when direct click attribution is lost.

## 7. Citation Velocity

This is the ultimate predictive metric. Citation Velocity measures how rapidly your client's brand is being cited by highly authoritative third-party platforms (like Reddit, Forbes, or industry review sites).

*   **The Formula:** `(New citations in current period - New citations in prior period) / (New citations in prior period) * 100`
*   **Why it matters:** A healthy velocity (10-15% monthly growth) is a leading indicator that an AI model will soon recognize your client as a trusted entity.
